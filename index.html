FAQs

Common questions.

What does Self-Healing actually do? +
Our AI monitors every page, asset, script, API, and integration in real time. When it detects a failure, it diagnoses the root cause and applies a verified fix in a sandboxed deploy — before your visitors notice.
Can I keep my domain and brand assets? +
Yes. Custom domains with automatic SSL on every plan. Your fonts, colours, and media work out of the box. White-label options available on Professional and Enterprise.
How does migration work? +
Free, concierge migration on Professional and Enterprise. Your site is cloned, QA'd, then DNS-cut over. Done in 24 hours, zero downtime, guaranteed.
What if the AI applies a wrong fix? +
Every fix creates an immutable snapshot first. Verified in sandbox before promoting. One-click rollback to any prior state. Auto-resolution rate is 98.7%.
Is EarlBear suitable for regulated industries? +
Yes. SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, GDPR/CCPA standard. HIPAA available on Enterprise with signed BAA. EU data residency configurable on any paid plan.
Are there contracts or lock-ins? +
No. Cancel monthly plans anytime. Annual plans get 20% off and a 30-day money-back window. Enterprise terms are custom.
